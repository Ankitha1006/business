Top three activities to do at Bengaluru

1. Bannarghatta national park

Wildlife park featuring diverse animal encounters, including a safari through natural habitats and a serene butterfly park. Enjoyable buggy rides available.

2. ISKCON Temple

ISKCON Sri Radha Krishna temple was inaugurated in the year 1997. It is not just a temple, but a cultural complex housing the temples dedicated to the Deities of Sri Sri Radha Krishnachandra.

3. WONDERLA

Wonderla Holidays Limited, is the No. 1 amusement park operator in India. It has a wonderful and adventurous water and dry rides.
